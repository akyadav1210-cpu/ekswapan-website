About Ekswapan Enterprises

Welcome to Ekswapan Enterprises, your premier destination for high-quality imitation jewelry. Based in the heart of Jaipur, the city of gems, we are passionate about bringing you designs that blend traditional artistry with contemporary style.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to make beautiful, statement jewelry accessible to everyone. We believe that you don't need a royal budget to look and feel regal. Each piece in our collection, from stunning Kundan sets to elegant pendants, is crafted with meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to quality.

We work with skilled artisans who share our vision for excellence. Thank you for choosing Ekswapan Enterprises to be a part of your story. We hope our jewelry brings you as much joy as it brings us to create it.

Shipping & Returns

Shipping Policy

Processing Time: We strive to process and ship all orders within 2-3 business days.

Shipping Duration: Delivery times vary by location. Typically, orders arrive within 3-5 business days for metro cities and 5-7 business days for other locations across India.

Shipping Costs: We offer FREE shipping on all prepaid orders above ₹999. A flat shipping fee of ₹70 is applied to orders below this amount. Cash on Delivery (COD) is available for an additional charge of ₹50.

Order Tracking: Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email and an SMS with your tracking number and a link to track your package.

Return & Exchange Policy

7-Day Returns: We offer a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery. To be eligible for a return, the item must be in its original, unused condition, with all tags and packaging intact.

How to Initiate a Return: Please contact our customer support team at returns@ekswapan.com with your order ID and reason for return. Our team will guide you through the process.

Damaged or Incorrect Items: If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, please notify us within 48 hours of delivery with a photograph of the item. We will arrange for a replacement or a full refund at no extra cost to you.

Refunds: Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund. The amount will be credited back to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I care for my imitation jewelry?

To ensure your jewelry lasts long, keep it away from water, perfumes, and other chemicals. Store it in a dry, airtight box or the packaging provided. Always wipe it with a soft, dry cloth after use.

What materials are used in your jewelry?

Our jewelry is crafted from high-quality, skin-friendly brass and copper alloys. They are plated with gold or silver and adorned with semi-precious stones, beads, and crystals. All our products are nickel-free and lead-free, making them safe for sensitive skin.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, we will send you an email and an SMS containing your tracking number and a link to our courier partner's website, where you can see the live status of your delivery.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods, including Credit Cards, Debit Cards, UPI, Net Banking, and popular mobile wallets. We also offer a Cash on Delivery (COD) option for your convenience.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, Ekswapan Enterprises only ships within India. We are working on bringing our beautiful collections to customers worldwide very soon!
